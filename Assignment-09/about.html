About




Why Swatch?

Swatching can be meditative, relaxing, and happiness inducing. Through swatching pens, pencils, crayons, markers, highlighters, paints, and beyond, a rainbow plethora of visual engagement is created. There is both a psychological uplift to the artistic organization and personalization of it all and a practical benefit of having a reference for how art and writing supplies appear on various types of paper.




What advice do I have for beginners?

Approach swatching with excitement! Be happy as you collect your tools, and let go of any stress while you fully engage in the swatching process. Also, know that swatching is a completely personalizable art. Create a minimal, whimsical, medieval, rustic, or whatever style swatch you want! While there are general principles, these can be adapated to each individual's preferences. Also, explore different paper types (black, craft, construction, lasterjet, etc.) for a rounded perspective.
