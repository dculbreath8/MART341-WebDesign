Tuxedo Popcorn

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of freshly popped popcorn
  • 1/4th cup of white chocolate shavings
  • 1/4th cup of dark chocolate shavings

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 250oF.
  2. Place white chocolate shavings and dark chocolate shavings into two separate ramekins.
  3. Place ramekins in oven for five minutes.
  4. Do not let the chocolate stay in the oven for over ten mintues or else the sugar will burn.
  5. Remove the chocolate filled ramekins, and stir the liquid.
  6. Drizzle melted chocolate over freshly popped popcorn with whisk or spoon, and enjoy!
