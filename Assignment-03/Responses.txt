1. Computers utilize the Domain Name System (DNS) to associate IP addresses with standard internet addresses. 2. A cookie is a file that is store on a computer and is composed of data (somewhat resembling an ID card in function) as the purpose is to remember information about the specific device or linked account when enabled to do so.

3. The protocol, domain, and path are the three essential parts of a URL.
